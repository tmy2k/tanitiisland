Discover Dining on Taniti

Taniti offers a wide variety of dining experiences, from beachfront seafood restaurants to local cafés serving traditional island dishes. Whether you’re craving fresh seafood, tropical fruit, or international cuisine, the island has something to satisfy every taste. Explore the best dining options on Taniti and savor the flavors of this tropical paradise.

Not a Dine-Out Person?

For those who prefer to cook their own meals, Taniti has a well-stocked local grocery store offering fresh produce, island-grown fruits, and all the essentials for creating delicious meals. The Taniti Market is the perfect place to pick up local ingredients and products for your stay.
