Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most common questions visitors have about Taniti. If you have a question that isn't listed here, feel free to contact us!

What is the voltage of power outlets in Taniti?

Power outlets in Taniti are 120 volts, the same as in the United States.

Are there any restrictions on alcohol sales in Taniti?

Alcohol is not allowed to be served or sold between midnight and 9:00 a.m.

What is the drinking age in Taniti?

The legal drinking age in Taniti is 18. However, it is not strictly enforced.

Do people speak English in Taniti?

Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English. However, in rural areas, especially among older residents, English is not widely spoken.

Is crime a concern in Taniti?

Violent crime is very rare in Taniti. However, as tourism increases, there have been reports of pickpocketing and other petty crimes.

What currency is used in Taniti?

Taniti uses the U.S. dollar as its official currency. Many businesses also accept euros and yen. Several banks offer currency exchange, and major credit cards are widely accepted.
